About Us

Startup world cup Nepal regional competition is the all Nepal regional competition brought in this region by startup hub Nepal and World trade Group Nepal to foster the local innovation to reach the global platform. It is a global competition which provide chance to meet global startups, provides access to new deals all around the world, helps to build network with investors and corporate representatives and win the grand price of $1 million. Startups, Corporate companies and investors can join this competition event and get the benefits of networking, investment opportunities, meet the experts of the field.
